Player Guide

Use the accordion below for quick help with organizing your collection and understanding what each tracker section does.

Open the library manager section, enter a game title, choose a status, set the progress percentage, and add an optional note or image URL. When you submit the form, a new game card is added instantly to your tracker.

Playing means the title is in your current rotation, Backlog means you plan to play it later, and Completed means you finished your current goals for that game. These labels also change the badge and progress bar colors for quick visual organization.

GameVault combines images, notes, status badges, and progress bars, all in one responsive layout. It gives you a cleaner and more engaging way to manage your collection than a simple text list.
